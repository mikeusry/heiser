# Heiser Group Customer Personas

**Prepared for:** The Heiser Group
**Prepared by:** point.dog
**Date:** December 2024

---

## Executive Summary

These four customer personas represent the primary audiences for The Heiser Group's cleaning services. Each persona is based on the company's service offerings, geographic market (Lake County IL, Chicago North Shore, Kenosha WI), and 45+ years of serving these communities.

---

## Persona Overview

| Persona | Service Focus | Annual Value | Volume Potential |
|---------|--------------|--------------|------------------|
| [Busy Professional Barbara](01-busy-professional-barbara.md) | Residential (bi-weekly) | $2,400–$4,800 | High retention, referrals |
| [Office Manager Oliver](02-office-manager-oliver.md) | Commercial | $6,000–$24,000 | Multi-year contracts |
| [Realtor Rachel](03-realtor-rachel.md) | Move-in/out | $3,000–$12,000 | Volume referrals |
| [Special Occasion Sarah](04-special-occasion-sarah.md) | Deep clean / one-time | $300–$1,200 | Entry to recurring |

## Key Insights

### Barbara (Residential)
- **Biggest fear:** Unreliable service
- **Biggest desire:** Peace of mind
- **Key message:** "Same team, every time"

### Oliver (Commercial)
- **Biggest fear:** Getting blamed for vendor failures
- **Biggest desire:** A partner who handles it
- **Key message:** "Your facilities partner, not just a vendor"

### Rachel (Realty)
- **Biggest fear:** Missing a closing deadline
- **Biggest desire:** Speed and availability
- **Key message:** "Move-in ready, fast"

### Sarah (Special Occasion)
- **Biggest fear:** Paying for "deep clean" that isn't
- **Biggest desire:** Visible results
- **Key message:** "When regular cleaning won't cut it"
