Barbara is a high-achieving professional who excels at work but feels guilty about the state of her home. She and her spouse both work demanding jobs, leaving little energy for household maintenance. She's willing to pay premium prices for peace of mind. What she wants most is **reliability**—knowing that her home will be clean when she gets home, without having to think about it.
