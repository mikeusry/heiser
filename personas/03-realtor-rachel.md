# Realtor Rachel

> High-volume agent who needs a fast, reliable cleaning partner to make listings shine

**Annual Value:** $3,000–$12,000
**Primary Service:** Move-in/move-out and pre-listing cleaning

---

## Demographics

| Attribute | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| Age | 28–55 |
| Sales Volume | 15–50+ transactions/year |
| Territory | Lake County IL, North Shore, Kenosha WI |
| Brokerage | Major brand or boutique firm |
| Role | Residential real estate agent, sometimes property manager |

---

## Who She Is

Rachel is always juggling multiple deals. She doesn't have time for vendors who create problems. She needs people who just handle things. A great cleaner makes her look good—a bad one costs her deals and reputation.

She's built a network of reliable vendors (photographers, stagers, inspectors, cleaners) and treats them as extensions of her team. When she finds someone good, she refers constantly.

---

## Pain Points

1. Cleaners who can't turn around jobs fast enough
2. Inconsistent quality between different properties
3. Coordinating schedules with closings and showings
4. Clients complaining about dirty move-in condition
5. Deposit disputes over cleaning issues

---

## Decision Triggers

- Lost a deal because property wasn't ready
- Staging company needs clean space first
- Move-out inspection found issues
- Building a team of reliable vendors
- Opening new market area (Lake County, Kenosha)
