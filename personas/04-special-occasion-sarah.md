## Who She Is

Sarah isn't looking for ongoing service (yet). She has a specific situation that requires professional help. Maybe she's hosting a big event, just had construction done, or is selling her home. She's willing to pay for a thorough job but is nervous about hiring for a one-time project.

She's done her own cleaning for years and has specific standards. She needs to understand exactly what she's getting and feel confident it'll meet her expectations.

---

## Pain Points

1. Don't know where to start with a big cleaning project
2. Regular cleaning won't cut it for this situation
3. Worried about hiring strangers for a one-time job
4. Previous deep clean wasn't actually deep
5. Need results fast with a deadline approaching
